Basic info about Subnautica

As said before, to escape the planet you need to cure yourself. The cure is hard to find, as you need to explore underwater alien bases. In there you will find information about what could be a cure, as well as info on the planets many lifeforms. The most dangerous creatures are the Leviathan class creatures, because they are enormous and except for one species, they will try to kill you. After you have found a cure you can disable the thing that keeps you on the planet and you can build a rocket ship that will send you to the nearest stargate, where you can get transport back to where you came from. You need to stay alive by eating fish that are swimming around. The most common alien fish is the peeper and its associates. Useful fish near your lifepod are for instance the bladderfish, which gets you water with the fabricator, and other small fish you can eat.

There are many dangers in the game, and the biggest threat are the predators. You have small ones like biters, medium ones like the stalkers, but also enormous ones like the Reaper Leviathan. This creature can even destroy the vehicles you build, so the best way to not die is to avoid him and other Leviathans.
