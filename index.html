What is this site about?

This site is about the game subnautica. It is an open world survival game made by Unknown Worlds Entertainment where you strand on an alien planet full of water, which is full of life. You need to gather materials in order to build a base, submarines and more. You are infected with a virus know as the Carar-virus.This makes you sick and you need to find a cure in order to escape the planet.
